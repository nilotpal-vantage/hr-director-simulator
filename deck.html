The Problem

HR content competes on volume.

Most VC blog content is consumed once and forgotten. HR Directors face real-stake decisions every quarter — and have no safe space to test their thinking.

Competitors offer quizzes, checklists, and assessments. None simulate a year of decisions end-to-end.

"What would I do if I had to make twelve real HR Director calls in a year?" — there's no tool that answers that. Until now.

What the Player Does

Four-step flow.

Step 1 · Setup

Set the stage

Pick industry, company stage, starting condition. Three dropdowns. Done in 20 seconds.

Step 2 · Play

Twelve decisions

Each has four options with real trade-offs. Stats shift live. No obvious right answers.

Step 3 · Result

The reveal

One of five archetypes. Final stats vs benchmarks. Share-ready card.

Step 4 · Take Home

The report

Five-page personalized PDF. Includes counterfactuals — "if you'd chosen X instead, your score would be Y."

HR Director Simulator · CTO PitchSlide 5
The Five Archetypes

Recognizable role types.

  • The Visionary  ·  Strategic, boardroom-led. Made recognition a board metric.
  • The Champion  ·  Equity-first, culture-deep. Lifted everyone fairly.
  • The Pragmatist  ·  Balanced, defensive. Workable foundation to compound on.
  • The Bureaucrat  ·  Process-led, low-warmth. Dashboards look good; hallways are quiet.
  • The Burnout  ·  Survived a year of crisis. Depleted but standing.

Each archetype maps to a curated five-post VC blog reading list. Every PDF surfaces them.

The Strategic Case

Three plays in one.

Depth

An HR Director is far more likely to share a tool that tells them who they are than another listicle.

Lead Capture

Email gate at peak engagement — right between the result reveal and the PDF download.

Content Amplification

Every PDF embeds five VC blog recommendations matched to the player's archetype. Drives blog traffic.

Most content tools do one of these. This does all three from a single interaction.

Path to Production

Three small phases.

  • Phase 1 · ~3 dev-days

    Backend for email + lead capture

    Light Node service. Catches email, posts to HubSpot, triggers email with PDF attached.

  • Phase 2 · ~3 dev-days

    Real benchmark data

    Store completed games in a small DB. Compute real averages + percentiles. Replace mocks.

  • Phase 3 · ~1–2 weeks

    Polish + amplification

    Real LinkedIn share card, sharper onboarding, mobile polish, optional Year 2 expansion.
